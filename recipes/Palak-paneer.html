Palak Paneer Recipe

Description

Palak Paneer is a classic North Indian dish made with spinach and Indian cottage cheese (paneer). It's flavorful, creamy, and packed with nutrients.

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 500g spinach leaves (washed and chopped)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 2 tbsp cream or yogurt (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee

Steps

  1. Blanch the spinach in boiling water for 2 minutes, drain and blend into a smooth puree.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies; cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Add tomato puree, turmeric, coriander powder, and salt; cook until oil separates.
  6. Add spinach puree and cook for 5-7 minutes on medium heat.
  7. Add paneer cubes and cook for another 5 minutes.
  8. Sprinkle garam masala and add cream or yogurt if using; mix well.
  9. Serve hot with roti or rice.
