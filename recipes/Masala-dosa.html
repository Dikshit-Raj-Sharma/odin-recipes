Masala Dosa Recipe

Description

Masala Dosa is a popular South Indian crispy crepe made from fermented rice and urad dal batter, stuffed with spicy potato filling.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 2 large potatoes (boiled and mashed)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 green chilies (finely chopped)
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp chana dal (optional)
  • 1 tsp urad dal (optional)
  • Few curry leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking

Steps

  1. Soak rice and urad dal separately for 6-8 hours.
  2. Grind soaked rice and dal to make a smooth batter; ferment overnight.
  3. Heat oil, add mustard seeds, chana dal, urad dal, and curry leaves; saut√©.
  4. Add onions and green chilies; cook until onions turn translucent.
  5. Add turmeric powder, salt, and mashed potatoes; mix well to make the filling.
  6. Heat a non-stick pan, pour a ladle of batter, spread thinly to make a dosa.
  7. Drizzle oil around edges, cook until crispy and golden brown.
  8. Place potato filling on dosa, fold and serve hot with chutney and sambar.
